## About

I am an AI Engineer and Computer Science student at Nile University focused on building reliable, production-oriented Generative AI systems. My work combines software engineering, LLM applications, retrieval-augmented generation, model fine-tuning, backend APIs, databases, and modern frontend development.

I enjoy working at the intersection of AI research and real product engineering. I care about building systems that are not only intelligent, but also grounded, testable, scalable, and useful for real users. My strongest technical areas include Python, PyTorch, Hugging Face, LangChain, RAG pipelines, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, React, and full-stack product integration.

My current focus is on LLM-powered products, AI safety validation, retrieval systems, fine-tuned model workflows, and end-to-end AI platforms that solve practical problems.

Added a validator that recalculates calories and macros to enforce targets and reduce hallucination risk.
